The Beginning

We met at college and quickly bonded over music and started going to gigs together! It wasn't long until we started to make music, mostly playing little ditties we made up. Things started to get a bit more serious and we started to look for a rehearsal space. We were in luck.. David's dad had a garage and the neighbours didn't mind! Next we needed a name, we took inspiration from Suzy's childhood cat who had just been confirmed as 100% deaf by the vet! We started playing at open mic nights, a lot of the bars asked us to come and play bigger sets! So we had to learn a lot more songs...

Our first EP

We were playing somewhere most weekends, but we wanted to play our own music! We got to writing and it all flowed so naturally.. When we were starting to think about recording a good friend of ours reccomended a producer who really helped us find our sound! We are forever grateful. We released it digitally at first, but we had lots of requests for record imprints. The small batch were all sold before we had them made! We never expected to be so in demand, a full album followed 6 months later to rave reviews!
